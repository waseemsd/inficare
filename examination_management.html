Examination Management System (EMS)

The Examination Management System popularly known as RESO (Result Software) is meticulously designed to simplify and automate the entire process of result preparation in complete compliance with the rules and regulations of Mumbai University and other academic boards. It ensures accurate mark calculation, effortless data management, and seamless report generation — transforming the traditionally complex and time-consuming examination process into a smooth and error-free operation.

Key Features

  • Automated Result Preparation

    The system handles mark conversions, total calculations, and generation of marksheets automatically, ensuring high accuracy and full adherence to university and board norms. What was once a stressful and tedious task now becoming a streamlined and efficient process.

  • Flexible Configuration

    Provision to modify Courses, Exams, and Groups during operation, offering complete flexibility for institutions to manage last-minute changes or academic restructuring without disrupting data consistency.

  • Comprehensive MIS Reports

    Generate detailed Management Information System (MIS) reports such as Class & Subject Toppers and Class & Subject Summaries to provide valuable academic insights and performance analytics at a glance.

  • Automatic Grace Marks Calculation

    The system intelligently calculates and applies grace marks wherever applicable, based on predefined university or institutional rules, ensuring fairness and compliance.

  • Automated ATKT Form Generation

    Effortlessly generate ATKT (Allowed To Keep Terms) forms for eligible students, minimizing manual intervention and ensuring timely documentation.

  • Flexible Marks Entry Options

    Marks can be entered course-wise or student-wise, providing flexibility and ease of operation for faculty and administrative staff.

  • Advanced Printing Options

    Supports printing of various examination reports including:
    • Exam-wise and Course-wise Reports
    • Gazette and multiple MIS reports
    • Hall Tickets and Grade Cards
    These can be printed on both pre-printed stationery and plain paper as per institutional preference.

  • Photo Attachment Facility

    Option to attach student photographs on hall tickets, grade cards, or reports to enhance authenticity and identification.

  • Mobile App Integration

    Students can easily access their examination results through the mobile app, allowing instant viewing of academic performance and ensuring complete digital transparency. The app also provides a convenient option to pay ATKT fees online, making the entire process faster and hassle-free.
