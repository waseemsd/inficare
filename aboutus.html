Inficare Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

Formerly known as Infinite Software Solutions

Empowering Institutions. Enhancing Efficiency. Enabling Growth.

Established in October 1999, Inficare Solutions Pvt. Ltd. has been a trusted technology partner delivering innovative software solutions that align with its vision - “Look Beyond the Possible.”

With deep domain expertise and a commitment to excellence, Inficare began its journey by developing specialized Result Processing Software. Over the years, the company has evolved into a comprehensive solutions provider, driven by innovation, teamwork, and customer-centric values.

In 2004, Inficare expanded its services by introducing Result Processing as a Process Outsourcing solution, empowering small and medium-sized educational institutions to adopt computerized and efficient result management systems.

Recognizing the growing need for cloud-based solutions, Inficare introduced Whiiz ERP in 2021 — a comprehensive platform designed for higher education institutions. The software has since evolved into a fully cloud-based ERP system, offering enhanced accessibility, scalability, and performance.

Today, Inficare Solutions offers a suite of software solutions designed to streamline business processes, enhance productivity, and optimize communication across institutions. Our products and services deliver measurable results — improving operational performance while reducing costs and complexity.
 

Inficare Solutions Pvt. Ltd. continues to grow with a focus on innovation, reliability, and value-driven technology, helping organizations stay ahead in a rapidly changing digital landscape.

Vision

To be the preferred technology partner for forward-thinking organizations by transforming technology into a strategic business advantage through collaboration, innovation, and excellence.

Mission

To foster a culture of fairness, integrity, and respect in all our interactions — with customers, employees, partners, and society at large — while delivering value-driven technology solutions that empower growth and success.
